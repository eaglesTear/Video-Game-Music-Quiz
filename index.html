Videogame OST Quiz

How well do you know your videogame music?

How to Play

When ready, click the Start button to begin the game / quiz. Read the questions, click the buttons in each question box to listen to a piece of video game music, then select the answer you think is correct. Hints are available for you to use.

You have 90 seconds to complete the quiz. When you are done, click Finish to tally your score, but WATCH OUT: if the counter reaches 0, your score will also be 0!
